About this page

This site hosts two publicly testable AI agents designed to support cross-cultural communication for students and professionals engaging with Italy. Visitors are invited to ask questions, compare responses, and help us evaluate usability, clarity, and practical usefulness.

One agent focuses on cultural communication and everyday social norms, while the other focuses on business culture and professional expectations. Together, they are meant to act less like generic chatbots and more like specialized guides for a defined cross-cultural task.

What we hope testers will try

We encourage visitors to ask practical, comparative, and scenario-based questions. You might try workplace or etiquette questions, ask about regional differences, test how the agents handle ambiguity, or compare the two Giulias on the same prompt to see whether they stay in their lanes.

Good questions to test

Examples: "How formal should I be in an email to an Italian manager?" or "Would this behavior come across differently in Milan versus Bologna?"

Things we care about

Accuracy, clarity, usefulness, tone, whether the agent stays within scope, and whether its answer feels more grounded than a typical general-purpose LLM.

Giulia C.

Giulia C. is an interactive AI guide for Italian culture and communication. She is designed to help users better understand everyday social expectations in Italy, including conversational style, etiquette, nonverbal cues, and culturally specific interaction norms.

We will only be able to access information you provide. Please avoid sharing personal, sensitive, or identifying information. Read our transparency notice.

Giulia B.

Giulia B. is an interactive AI guide for Italian business culture and professional communication. She is designed to help users prepare for workplace interactions in Italian contexts, including meetings, expectations around professionalism, communication style, and cross-cultural business etiquette.
